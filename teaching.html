Current Teaching

Due to my current post as Senior Research Scientist, I am not giving classes on a regular basis. In one of my previous posts as associate professor at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (Barcelona, Spain), I had the normal teaching load of a Spanish university professor.

Courses on Metaheuristics and Hybrids

Occasionally, I give courses of 20-40 hours on metaheuristics and hybrid techniques for optimization (in English or Spanish). The last course in Spanish took place at the University of Cuenca, Ecuador, October 2021. And the last one in English took place at the Vienna Graduate School on Computational Optimization, Austria, March 2022.
